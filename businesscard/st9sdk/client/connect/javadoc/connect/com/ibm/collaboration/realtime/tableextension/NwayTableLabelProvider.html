extends TableLabelProvider

A label provider for tables used in an n-way chat window participant list. Used with the com.ibm.collaboration.realtime.chatwindow.nwayListExtension extension point.

Note: The getColumnText and getColumnImage methods are called assuming that the index of the columns are zero-based according to the additions that you made. So if you added two new columns to the participant list, you would check for index 0 and 1, regardless of where your columns are located in the full table.
