                      throws TelephonyServiceException

Description copied from interface: VoiceChatDeviceController
Plays the incoming ring sound on the ring device, if supported by the service provider. The ring sound plays until stopped using VoiceChatDeviceController.stopIncomingRing(). Use VoiceChatDeviceController.setIncomingRingSound(String) and VoiceChatDeviceController.setRingDevice(String) to specify, respectively, the incoming ring sound and ring device
