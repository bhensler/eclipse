Interface ClickToCallServiceProvider

All Known Implementing Classes:
AbstractClickToCallServiceProvider
Deprecated. No longer used as of Sametime 8.5. 

public interface ClickToCallServiceProvider
extends CallServiceProvider

ClickToCallServiceProvider is implemented by the telephony plug-in that provides services used by the Sametime Connect click-to-call feature. The click-to-call plug-in included with Sametime Connect uses services provided by a Telephony Conferencing SPI (TCSPI) plug-in installed on the Sametime server.

Since:
Sametime 7.5.1 (replaces ThirdPartyCallServiceProvider)
