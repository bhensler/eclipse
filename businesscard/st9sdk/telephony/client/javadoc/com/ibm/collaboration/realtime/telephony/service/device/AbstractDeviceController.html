@Deprecated
public abstract class AbstractDeviceController
extends AbstractDeviceNotifier
implements DeviceController

AbstractDeviceController and its subclasses provide default implementations of certain methods in the DeviceController interface and its subinterfaces. The abstract classes do not provide implementations for methods that must be implemented by the service provider. Service providers who implement a device controller class should extend the appropriate abstract class, both to take advantage of the default method implementations, and to allow the interfaces to evolve in future releases without breaking existing implementations.

Since:
Sametime 7.5.1
