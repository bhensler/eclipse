com.ibm.collaboration.realtime.telephony.device
Interface CallForwardingOptions

Deprecated. For internal use only starting with Sametime 8.5.2. 

@Deprecated
public interface CallForwardingOptions

CallForwardingOptions represents call forwarding options used by phone devices. The options are based on the ECMA uaCSTA (a.k.a. TR/87) standard.

Since:
Sametime 7.5.1
