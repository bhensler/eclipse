AbstractIncomingCallHandler
public interface IncomingCallHandler

IncomingCallHandler is used by external plug-ins to handle incoming calls before Sametime Connect processes them. The incoming call handler can handle the incoming call itself, tell Sametime Connect to automatically accept or reject the call, or let Sametime Connect process the call in the normal way. Use CallManager.addIncomingCallHandler(IncomingCallHandler) to register an incoming call handler.

Since:
Sametime 8.0.1
