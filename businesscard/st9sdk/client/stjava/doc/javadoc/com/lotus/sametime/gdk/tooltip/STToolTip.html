      • setHoverBased

        void setHoverBased(boolean hover_based)
        Deprecated. 
        Sets wether or not this STToolTip object is entry based or hover based. Entry based STTooltip objects display their tooltips at a specified time interval after the initial entry of the mouse into that component regardless of any mouse movement that occurs after the entry. Hover based tooltips will display their tooltips at a specified time interval after the mouse has stopped moving within the STTooltip's Component.
